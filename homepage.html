Connect With Travelers Around The World

Our website is dedicated to the bold and brave at heart. Many people want to travel the world, but sometimes finances, not knowing anyone, and not knowing the area can get into the way of that. Flights from the United States to Europe and vice versa are at some of the lowest prices they've ever been, and are sometimes cheaper than domestic flights. A major cost of traveling is often a place to stay however. This is where this platform comes in. We aim to connect users in one country to another by offering a website for them to become friends, and to eventually share their home with a new friend. People whose place of living and desired place of visitng coincide can now visit each other. Technology is meant to connect the people of the world, and this is an avenue to do so.
